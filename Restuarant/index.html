About Us


Born from family recipes and simmering dreams, Take It Away isn't just a restaurant, it's a legacy. Our story began years ago, in kitchens fragrant with spices and alive with the warmth of loved ones. Now, we bring those cherished flavors to you, offering a taste of India's rich heritage. But our journey doesn't end there. We're constantly innovating, exploring new avenues while staying true to our roots. Like a well-seasoned dish, we evolve, but the core remains the same: sharing authentic Indian fare cooked with love. So, join us on this culinary adventure. Savor the past, relish the present, and discover the delicious future of Tikka Take It Away.
